Delivering mowed lawns, clean garages, and walked dogs.
Welcome to a platform where finding employment for the jobs you don't have time for can happen in seconds.
Get ready for rapid, convenient, job completion.
1
Regular people post jobs.
Regular people post jobs they would like to get done, but don't have time for. They allocate a wage, a location, and description for the job.
2
Sapharii curates employers.
Sapharii matches employers of backgrounds pertaining to the job description and adds it to their feed.
3
Employees discover those jobs.
Individuals looking for jobs get to choose which ones they would like to complete from their feed. Employers are notified of their new employees.
4
Jobs are completed rapidly.
Sending the same job to hundreds of employers, jobs get completed very fast. Transactions are completely handled by Sapharii.
One account, two modes
Sapharii allows anyone to be both an employer or employee. Switch between a hiring mode or a working mode from one account to get the most out of the platform.
